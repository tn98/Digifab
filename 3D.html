MANDATORY TASKS

I have chosen to make a device holder, more specific a mobile holder for my iPhone 12. I first made the drawings by hand and then continued to make the drawings in Autodesk Fusion. I took the measurements from apple official site “https://www.apple.com/no/iphone-12/specs/”.
The reason why I made a mobile holder is that I need one for my personal use. For example, this will be useful when making food and watching a series at the same time. Or when I lay down and are too lazy to hold the phone.
Another thing is the holder here can also be printed out to other devices such as tablets. The only thing that is needed is to scale up the figure and maybe take more infill of plastic to make it more stable.
